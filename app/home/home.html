Welcome to my Countries + Capitals App

The app consists of 2 different views:

Browse Countries:

You find a list of all the major countries in the world with:

Name / Country Code / Capital / Area in km2 / Population / Continent

Country Detail:

If you click on a country, you are presented with a detailed view of that country with:

Name / Population / Area in km2 / Capital / Population of Capital / Up to 3 Neighbors / Map + Flag

You always find buttons to go back to home or the country overview.

Enjoy!
